### 👋 About me

I'm a 2nd-year Computer Engineering student at **ENICarthage** (Tunisia), building at the intersection of **AI agents**, **full-stack systems**, and the occasional **industrial hardware** rabbit hole.

- 🧠 Designing multi-engine **LangChain / LangGraph** pipelines (`InfluBoost`)
- 🏛️ Shipping production platforms for my school (`EniGov`, deployed at ENICarthage)
- ⚙️ Building ERPs, dashboards, and delivery-tracking systems end to end
- 🔩 Currently deep in a **G41/G42 cutter-radius-compensation pre-processor** for a grblHAL CNC sender — offline G-code transformation, segment offsetting, arc intersection, the works
- 🌍 Working fluently across **French / English**, remote-first
